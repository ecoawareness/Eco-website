Our story

Built by students, for students.

Climate action in Qatar is fragmented. Opportunities live in scattered WhatsApp groups. Volunteer hours get lost in paper forms. Students don't know where to start, and organizations can't find the passionate youth they need.

We saw this firsthand. As students ourselves, we watched friends give up on projects because they couldn't verify their work, and others miss opportunities they would've loved — simply because no one told them.

That's why we built EcoAwareness. One profile. Every opportunity. Verified hours. Real recognition. A single home for Qatar's climate generation.
