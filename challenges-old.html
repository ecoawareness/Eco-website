HOW IT WORKS

From posted problem to paid solution.

Four steps. Two to four weeks. One real deliverable for a real organization — think hackathon meets consulting project, running continuously.

01

A challenge goes live.

An organization posts a real sustainability challenge with a bounty attached.

02

Squad up.

Form a squad of 3–4 students — or join one — and apply for the challenge.

03

Build with an AI PM.

Your squad gets an AI project manager that plans tasks, tracks milestones, and keeps you on schedule.

04

Deliver. Win. Verify.

Submit your solution. Winners get the bounty and present to the org — everyone earns verified Eco ID hours.
