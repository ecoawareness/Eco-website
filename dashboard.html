Qatar's Climate Journey

Qatar's National Vision 2030 commits to balancing economic growth with environmental stewardship. The National Environment and Climate Change Strategy (NECCS) sets targets for reducing carbon intensity, expanding renewable energy to 20% by 2030, and protecting critical ecosystems like Al Thakira's mangroves and the Arabian Gulf's coral reefs.

Frequently Asked Questions.

Everything you need to know about EcoAwareness.

Getting Started
EcoAwareness is Qatar's climate action platform connecting passionate youth with NGOs, eco-businesses, student clubs, and government bodies. We make it easy to discover opportunities, track your impact, and grow your environmental network.
EcoAwareness is open to all youth in Qatar aged 15–30 who are passionate about the environment, climate action, or sustainability. Whether you're a student, young professional, or activist — there's a place for you here.
Yes — completely free. EcoAwareness is a non-profit initiative dedicated to growing Qatar's climate generation. All features including Opportunities, your Eco ID, and EcoBot are available at no cost.
Opportunities
Browse the Opportunities section, find something that interests you, and click 'Apply Now'. You'll fill in a short form with your details. The hosting organisation will contact you directly — most applications get a response within 3–5 business days.
Yes! Use the 'Post Event' button in the top bar. After a quick review by our team (usually within 24 hours), your event goes live for the entire EcoAwareness community to see.
Eco ID
Your Eco ID is a digital membership card that proves your involvement in Qatar's climate movement. It includes your name, role, member since date, and a unique QR code. You can share it digitally or show it at events — it's your green identity.
The Eco ID is recognised by our partner organisations and serves as proof of your engagement in Qatar's environmental sector. Several partner NGOs and government bodies accept it as a credentialing document for volunteer programmes.
Account & Privacy
Go to My Profile from the sidebar. You can update your display name, bio, skills, and interests at any time. Your Eco ID updates automatically to reflect any changes.
Absolutely. EcoAwareness never sells or shares your personal data with third parties. Your information is only used to personalise your experience and facilitate connections with partner organisations you choose to engage with.
To delete your account, send us an email at ecoawarenessme@gmail.com with the subject 'Delete Account'. We'll process your request within 7 business days and permanently remove all your data from our systems.
