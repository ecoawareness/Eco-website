Zayed Sustainability Prize — Global High Schools

UAE Government · Abu Dhabi
Hosted in the UAE, this is one of the most highly funded and prestigious environmental awards in the world. It is the ultimate launchpad for scaling physical smart infrastructure — such as AI-powered training stations, solar-integrated systems, or resource monitors — from a digital prototype to a fully deployed community system.
