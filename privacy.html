Legal

Your privacy, simply explained.

Last updated: April 23, 2026

We built EcoAwareness to help Qatari students track real climate impact — not to profile them, sell their data, or follow them around the internet. This page explains exactly what we collect, why, and what you control.

What we collect

When you create an account we collect your name, email address, a password (stored hashed), and — if you choose to add them — your school, age group, and climate interests. When you join opportunities we record the event, organizer, and hours logged. That's it.

We never collect:

  • Your location in the background
  • Your phone contacts
  • Biometric or facial data
  • Payment or financial information

Why we collect it

To run the platform. That means: verifying you're a real person, giving you a profile, matching you with opportunities that fit your interests, logging your verified hours, and generating your Eco ID certificate when you reach milestones.

Who sees your data

By default: only you and the EcoAwareness team. Organizers of events you attend see your name and the fact that you attended. Nothing else is shared unless you explicitly choose to.

We never sell your data. We don't run ad networks. We don't share profiles with third-party data brokers.

Where it's stored

Your data lives in Supabase, an encrypted Postgres database, with regular backups. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — even we can't read them. Connections to our site are encrypted via HTTPS.

Cookies

We use one cookie: a session cookie that keeps you logged in. We don't use tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or advertising cookies. Signing out clears the cookie.

Your rights

  • See your data. Request a full export anytime.
  • Fix your data. Edit your profile directly in your dashboard.
  • Delete your account. Email us and we'll wipe your data within 30 days.
  • Say no. You can withdraw consent for non-essential processing at any time.

Under 18

We welcome students of all ages. If you're under 13, please ask a parent or guardian before creating an account. Parents can email us anytime to review or delete a minor's data.

Changes to this policy

If we change anything significant, we'll email you and post a notice on the site before it takes effect. The date at the top of this page always reflects the most recent update.

Questions?

Email us at privacy@ecoawareness.qa. A real person on our team will reply within 5 business days.
