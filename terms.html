Legal

The ground rules, in plain English.

Last updated: April 23, 2026

By using EcoAwareness you're agreeing to these terms. They're short because we wrote them ourselves, for students. If anything is unclear, email us — we'll actually answer.

Who can use EcoAwareness

Anyone interested in climate action in Qatar — students, schools, NGOs, and companies are all welcome. If you're under 13 we need a parent or guardian's permission before you create an account.

Your account

One account per person. Keep your password secret — if someone logs in as you, anything they do counts as you. Tell us immediately if you think your account was compromised.

What you can do

  • Browse opportunities and sign up for ones you want to join
  • Log verified volunteer hours backed by organizers
  • Share your Eco ID certificate with anyone you want
  • Connect with other climate-minded students in your area

What you can't do

  • Log hours for events you didn't actually attend
  • Impersonate another person, organization, or school
  • Use EcoAwareness to harass, discriminate against, or intimidate anyone
  • Scrape, copy, or resell platform data without permission
  • Try to break, exploit, or abuse the platform's security

If you do any of these we'll warn you, and if it keeps happening we'll suspend or delete your account. Serious violations may be reported to your school or the appropriate authorities.

Verified hours

Hours become "verified" only when an organizer confirms you were there. Unverified hours still appear on your profile but are clearly marked. Your Eco ID certificate only counts verified hours — that's what makes it worth something.

Content you post

Anything you write on the platform — profile bios, event reviews, comments — stays yours. You just give us permission to display it on EcoAwareness. We may remove content that violates these terms.

Our stuff

The EcoAwareness name, logo, design, and code belong to us. You can screenshot, link to us, and talk about us — but don't copy our brand or clone the product.

Things break sometimes

We work hard to keep the platform running, but we can't promise zero downtime. We're not liable for lost opportunities caused by outages, bugs, or things outside our control. We'll always do our best to fix things fast and be transparent about what happened.

Changes to these terms

If we change anything significant we'll email you and post a notice on the site at least 30 days before the change takes effect. If you don't agree with the change, you can close your account.

Governing law

These terms are governed by the laws of the State of Qatar. If there's a disagreement we can't work out together, it'll be resolved in the courts of Doha.

Questions?

Email us at hello@ecoawareness.qa. We read every message.
